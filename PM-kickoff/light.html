Governance Definitions

Functional Team Roles

Clear definitions to ensure alignment across all workstreams.

💼

Project Manager

Responsible for facilitating communication and coordination between the client and the project team. Ensures seamless information flow, addresses inquiries, and manages key interactions to keep the project aligned with objectives and timelines.

📈

Commercial Service

Oversees business operations related to sales, customer service, contracts, and financial discussions. Ensures smooth commercial processes, manages client relationships, and supports business growth through efficient contract execution.

🔑

Delivery Owner

Key member responsible for overseeing and ensuring successful project delivery. Also known as the Project Lead or Coordinator, manages execution, aligns resources, tracks progress, and ensures objectives, timelines, and quality standards are met.

🛡️

Delivery Oversight

Supports the Delivery Owner in ensuring successful project execution. Also known as the Secondary Delivery Owner, provides oversight, assists with deliverables, and steps in when the Delivery Owner is unavailable to maintain continuity.

🧬

Technical Members

Primarily responsible for executing project tasks related to review, preparation, submission, and problem-solving. Contribute expertise to ensure successful completion of deliverables.

🪙

Accounts Receivable

Handles invoicing, reconciliations, payment receipts, credit notes, and related financial documentation. Ensures accounting issues are resolved promptly.

PM & Delivery Owner Responsibilities

Core accountabilities for seamless project execution.

📢

Expectation & Scope Mapping

Communicate client expectations, timelines, and define project deliverables or milestones as needed. Ensure project delivery aligns with the agreed-upon scope in the Statement of Work (SOW).

Continuous Status Tracking

Regularly provide the client with project progress updates and reports. Deliver frequent status updates to ensure transparency and stakeholder confidence.

Objective Realization

Ensure deliverables meet client expectations and project objectives. Oversee and manage tasks to meet deadlines and ensure smooth project progression.
